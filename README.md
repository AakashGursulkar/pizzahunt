# pizzahunt
This is the project of Pizza Hut. Here we can purchase a Pizza by online transaction.
Our project is useful for to buy the Pizza Shop by online system. There are Seven  forms included our project, such as: 
1 Home
2 About Pizza
3 Menu
4 Order
5 Order 1
6 Contact
7 Feedback
	In  home form shows different type of pizza such as VERDE, SIMPLY VEG.  In the about pizza history of Pizza shows.
